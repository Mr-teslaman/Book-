Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance can I book a bus ticket?

You can book bus tickets up to 90 days in advance through Tickify. This allows you to plan your travel well ahead of time and secure the best seats.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking through our website or mobile app. Cancellation policies vary by bus operator, so please check the terms before booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. All payments are processed through secure gateways.

How will I receive my ticket?

After successful payment, you will receive an e-ticket via email and SMS. You can also access it through your Tickify account.

Are there any hidden charges?

No, we believe in transparent pricing. The price you see during booking is the final amount you pay, with no hidden charges.
