About This Event

The Fashion Designer of the Year – Kisumu Edition is a regional fashion development event that showcases emerging designers after weeks of mentorship and creative challenges. Designers present their final collections on the runway, and top talents are awarded based on creativity, craftsmanship, and sustainability. The event also features the launch of a new KikoRomeo project supporting local designers.
